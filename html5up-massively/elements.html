ABOUT GLITTABOMB
FEFE

What is Glittabomb FeFe?

Glittabomb FeFe is a social media presence run by FeFe (age 16) that focuses on lots of different things! On YouTube, you can find DIY, coding, and my favorite--- stop motion animation videos! Other random videos (like vlogs!) are scattered in occasionally. FeFe's photography is located on Flickr and Instagram.

Why is that your name? Why the pug theme?

FeFe is my name, and I have no idea where "Glittabomb" came from but I love sparkles... so it works! I may or may not have a pug obsession... and have the two cutest pugs in the world, of course.

What's the story?

Since my dad brought home a little video camera when I was around 7, I have been obsessed with filmmaking. I remember the first videos I made were with Webkinz, and I did anything to get a cool shot (including throwing said camcorder out the window for a "bungee jumping" scene). Glittabomb FeFe was all started when I made a blogger blog in the seventh grade, at the age of 12. One day I decided to try to film an American Girl Stop Motion, and I ended up loving it. My parents then allowed me to publish videos to YouTube :D
